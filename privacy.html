Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2025

At Aditya Gaming Store, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our website, participate in our Free Fire tournaments, or purchase top-up services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Personal Information: Free Fire UID, name, email address, phone number (e.g., WhatsApp), and payment details (e.g., transaction IDs) when you register for tournaments or make purchases.
  • Contact Information: Email or WhatsApp number provided for tournament confirmations or customer support.
  • Payment Information: Transaction details processed through third-party payment platforms (Khalti, eSewa, Bank) for paid tournaments or top-ups.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent, collected via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Tournament Data: Squad details (e.g., UIDs) and match performance for tournament organization and prize distribution.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process tournament registrations and top-up purchases.
  • Send confirmations, tournament details (e.g., ID and password), and prize notifications via your chosen contact method (WhatsApp or Email).
  • Verify player identities and ensure fair play in tournaments.
  • Improve our website and services through analytics and user feedback.
  • Communicate updates, promotions, or support responses (with your consent).
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activities.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Third-Party Payment Processors: Khalti, eSewa, or banks to process payments, sharing only necessary transaction details.
  • Service Providers: Analytics or hosting providers (e.g., Formspree for form submissions) to operate our website and services.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights and users.
  • Tournament Organizers: Limited data (e.g., Free Fire UIDs) to manage matches and distribute prizes.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, such as:

  • Secure form submissions via HTTPS.

However, no online platform is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your Free Fire account details.

  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
